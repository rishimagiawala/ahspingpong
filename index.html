Our PURPOSE

The most important part of this club to enjoy and be able to play ping pong. We know how hard and stressful high school can be for some, and we want to provide a space, unlike school sports where students can simply play and enjoy instead of worrying about grades, ranks, or qualifying for various levels. Put simply, we aspire to build an environment where club members can practice, interact with others, and have a memorable experience all while playing the marvelous sport.

Frequently Asked Questions

 How Do I Sign Up?

First and most importantly, you need to fill out our signup form that can be accessed right here:

https://tinyurl.com/pingpsignup.

After that, make sure to signup to our communications like Remind and Groupme, which can be found in the Contact Info section.

 How Much Does It Cost To Join?

Our OSP member dues of $10 for new members (free for returning members) can be paid here: https://osp.osmsinc.com/FultonGA/BVModules/ProductTemplates/Bvc2013/Product.aspx?productid=EN701-183.

 Where and When Do We Meet Up?

All of our meetings will be held in the aux gym, where we can set up our six tables for play. As for times and dates, we are open to both morning and afternoon meetings depending on what our members' preferences are. Our main meetings will occur during anchor time, where we will meet up every Tuesday and Thursday during the designated Anchor Time period.
